FAQ

Do you use chemicals?

No. We prioritize manual removal with rakes, skimmers, and nets. If herbicide is requested, we refer to licensed partners and follow local regulations and permits.

Are you permitted and insured?

Yes. We are fully insured and permitted for aquatic plant removal in our service area. Documentation available upon request.

How should I prepare?

Ensure clear dock access and some space to stage bagged vegetation. We bring all gear and can dispose per local rules or your preference.

Will weeds grow back?

Manual removal reduces biomass and floating cuttings, which can slow regrowth. Seasonal plans and periodic touch-ups help maintain a clear swim area.
