Serving Madison & Dane County

Happy Shores proudly operates across all bodies of water in Dane County — including Lake Mendota, Lake Monona, Lake Waubesa, and Lake Kegonsa.

We take pride in our sustainable lakefront practices and our commitment to giving back. 10% of proceeds from every job and 25% of proceeds from our clothing line directly fund public shoreline cleanups and support conservation efforts to protect native fish populations throughout the Madison lakes.
